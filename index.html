Ingredients of Kopi Rempah

Immerse yourself in the enchanting allure of Toraja, South Sulawesi, through a sip of robusta blend. Our Kopi Rempah infuses the finest Toraja Robusta beans with a harmonious blend of ginger, cinnamon, clove, and nutmeg.

Breathe life into your mornings with the fiery kick of ginger, perfectly complementing the deep, roasted flavors of coffee. Ginger is a popular home remedy for nausea, digestion, and other health issues.

Savor the cozy warmth of our coffee blended with delicate hints of cinnamon, creating a harmonious cup that soothes and invigorates. Cinnamon is a spice derived from the bark of several tree species. It has antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects.

Experience the rich, earthy notes of nutmeg intertwined with our coffee for a delightful, comforting embrace in every sip. Nutmeg is known to have health benefits, including its ability to relieve pain, soothe indigestion, strengthen cognitive function, detoxify the body, boost skin health, and many more.

Elevate your daily ritual with the aromatic delight of clove-infused coffee. Each sip is an invitation to explore a world of complexity, where the earthy warmth of clove harmonizes with the rich, deep notes of coffee. Clove has proven medicinal benefits along with an abundance of a few essential nutrients. Clove is known to be a good antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, anti-cancer properties, anti-diabetic, natural pain-killer, and many more.

Experience the alluring sweetness of palm sugar, sourced from the heart of tropical palms. It's an authentic touch that elevates your coffee experience. Palm sugar has a lower glycemic index, which means it may have a milder impact on blood sugar levels. It contains antioxidants and minerals such as potassium, iron, zinc, and B vitamins.

Aer Guraka, ginger drink

AER GURAKA

is an herbal drink best served hot originally from Ternate, North Maluku. During the reign of the Sultanate of Ternate, this particular beverage was the noble families’ favorite and a special treat for imperial guests. Aer Guraka is made from a mixture of ginger, palm sugar, walnuts, and pandan leaves, making it special from any other Indonesian ginger drink.
